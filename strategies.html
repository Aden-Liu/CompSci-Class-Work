Strategies

Mortar is a card that can be difficult to use, especially if the opponent knows how to counter it.

Advantages

Mortar is a strong card on both offense and defense. Unlike its unpopular cousin, the X-bow, the main strategy with mortar decks is to slowly chip away at the opponent's tower.

You can set up a strong counter push if the opponent overcommits to get rid of your mortar.

It has a blind spot that allows it to attack the backline when placed on defense.

Disadvantages

Mortar is very easy to distract. Just place troops in the middle of the field or on the other side.

It attacks very slowly.
