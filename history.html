History of thy holy martor

Back

January 4th, 2016

Introduced to the game

February 2nd, 2016

Reduced cost from 6 Elixir to 4 Elixir

Damage reduced by 40%

Lifetime reduced from 40 seconds to 20 seconds

Range reduced from 13 tiles to 12 tiles

February 9th, 2016

Increased lifetime to 30 seconds

February 29th, 2016

Mortar would no longer stop its attack animation after the target died

Now unlocked in Builder's Workshop instead of Royal Arena

May 3rd, 2016

Increased the level cap of common cards from 12 to 13

Deployment time was increased from 3 seconds to 5 seconds

Damage was decreased by 10%

Decreased range from 5-12 tiles to 4.5-11 tiles(Effective range remained unchanged)

June 21st, 2016

Decreased Deployment time from 5 seconds to 4 seconds

December 15th, 2016

Increased the area radius from 1.8 tiles to 2 tiles

August 11th, 2017

Deployment time decreased from 4 seconds to 3.5 seconds

December 12th, 2017

Fixed a bug where the Mortar would not target troops close to its blindspot

January 24th, 2018

Decreased blindspot radius from 4.5 tiles to 3.5 tiles

April 25th, 2018

Changed the card's description. It used to read “Defensive building with a long range. Shoots exploding boulders that deal area damage. Cannot shoot at targets that get very close!”

June 20th, 2018

fixed a bug where the Mortar would occasionally stop shooting at the edge of its range.

July 7th, 2018

Damage Reduced by 3.5%

Hitpoints reduced by 4%

June 27th, 2022

Added the Siege Weapon Card Mastery task for the Mortar.

October 4th, 2022

Hitpoints decreased by 7%
